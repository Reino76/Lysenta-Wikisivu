Ancient Red Dragon

Legendary Creature — Dragon

Overview

The Ancient Red Dragon is among the most fearsome monsters of Dungeons & Dragons. Its fiery breath can level armies, while its cunning intellect makes it a master of schemes and dominion.

Lore

Red dragons are the most covetous of all dragonkind, claiming mountains and fiery wastelands as their dominion. They revel in destruction, amassing vast hoards of treasure and demanding tribute from mortals.

Abilities

Fire Breath (Recharge 5–6)

Exhales fire in a 90-foot cone. Each creature must make a DC 24 Dexterity saving throw, taking 91 (26d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Legendary Resistance (3/Day)

If the dragon fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

Frightful Presence

Each creature of the dragon’s choice within 120 ft. must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute.
